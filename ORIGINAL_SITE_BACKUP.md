## TRIBUTE PAGE (/tribute)

### Remembering Jean Missinhoun
Jean Missinhoun — Founder & Chairman of Earth Love United Foundation
- Passed unexpectedly on June 1, 2024, at age 52
- Originally from Benin, thrived as a hedge fund investor in London focused on energy investments
- Author of "The Green Energy Boom" (Barnes & Noble)
- Founded ELU in 2020
- Forged partnerships with nearly a dozen global organizations
- Initiated research into sustainable energy, natural carbon capture (hemp, mangroves, seagrass, algae)

**Succession**: Ekmel Ozdemir (General Manager since founding) serves as interim chairman.
